## Ingredients:
- 1 Cup Soy sauce
- &frac14; Cup Worchestorshire sauce
- &frac14; Cup Brown Sugar
- 3 cloves of garlic (minced)

## Instructions:
1. Throw all the chicken in a ziplock bag with the marinade, store in fridge, and use as needed.
