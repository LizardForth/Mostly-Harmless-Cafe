## Ingredients:
- 1 &frac12; cups milk
- 1 egg
- &frac14; cup sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- &frac14; tsp baking soda
- 1 Tbsp baking powder
- 2 Tbsp melted butter

## Instructions:
1. Pour onto medium heat pan.
1. Flip when bubbles appear on top
