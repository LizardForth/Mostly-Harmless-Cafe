Zesty, spicy (as in flavor, not heat) sauce. Good for fries and bratwurst.

## Ingredients
- 2 Tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 clove of garlic (grated)
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- &frac12; tsp curry powder
- &frac14; tsp salt
- &frac14; tsp pepper

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix.
