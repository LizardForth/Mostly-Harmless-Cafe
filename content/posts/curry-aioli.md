# Ingredients
- 2 Tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 clove of garlic (grated)
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- &frac12; tsp curry powder
- &frac14; tsp salt
- &frac14; tsp pepper

# Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix.
