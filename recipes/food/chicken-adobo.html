Chicken Adobo

Chicken:

  • 4 lbs chicken

Bone in, with skin.
Can be pretty much any part of chicken. It's nice to have diversity but if I had to choose a single part it would be chicken thighs because they're cheaper and fluffier.

Sauce:

  • 20 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 Tbsp black peppercorns
  • crack of black pepper
  • 3 Tbsp palm sugar (can use brown sugar)
  • 1¼ cup rice vinegar
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup dark soy sauce
  • ½ cup water

Instructions:

Estimated cook time: 45 minutes.

  1. Marinade chicken for two hours.
  2. Sear chicken, 2-3 minutes per side.
  3. Pour in marinade used in step one, bring to boi
  4. Simmer (with lid) 35 minutes.
  5. Flip chicken and cook for another 25 minutes.

Best served on rice.
