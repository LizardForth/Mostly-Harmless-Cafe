Cactus Ramen

  • 1pkt Maruchan® Ramen Noodle Soup

Instructions:

  1. Boil two cups of water in a saucepan, add noodles and cook for three minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Turn off heat, add contents of seasoning package, stir.
  3. Serve immediately for best results. Makes 2 8oz services.
