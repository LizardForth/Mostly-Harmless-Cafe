Choclate Chip Cookies.

Chicken:

  • ½ Cup butter
  • ½ Cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ Cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 Tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 1¾ Cups flour (all-purpose)
  • ½ Tsp baking soda
  • ½ Tsp kosher salt
  • 1 choclate chips (semi-sweet)

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 350°F

  1. Melt butter. Don't get the butter hot.
  2. Mix butter and both sugars in a large bowl. Make sure it's well combined
  3. Add vanilla to large bowl. Stir until mixed in well.
  4. Crack and mix in egg. Stir until well combined.
  5. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt to mixture. Adding to much flour will result in crumbling cookies.
  6. Mix Dough until soft and sticky. Avoid overly sticky dough.
  7. Mix in choclate chips. Avoid clumping them together.
  8. On baking sheet lined pan scoop out roughly 1½ sized dough balls and place around 2 inches appart (about 5cm).
  9. Bake for 7-10 minutes, or until they set. Start with a lower time if they need more time bake them longer. They should look puffy and slightly underbaked in the center.
