Spaghetti and Meatballs

Good for 1 lb spaghetti.

Meatballs:

  • ¼ lb ham (minced)
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp ground fennel seed
  • 1 ¾ tsp sea salt
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ¼ cup pecorino romano (fine shredded)
  • black pepper (from the hip)
  • ½ cup panko
  • 1 whole egg

Produces around 20 meatballs.

Sauce:

  • ¼ cup extra virgin olive oi
  • 4 cloves garlic (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 can (28 oz.) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of basil

This produces a somewhat thin coating of sauce. If you like having a really saucy pasta you can add 2 cans of crushed tomatoes. If you do this add another ½ tsp of salt.

Instructions (meatballs):

  1. Mix together all ingredient for meatballs in no particular order. Resulting mass should be consistent throughout and have a slight sponginess to it.
  2. Put in skillet on med-high, sear on all sides.
  3. Remove and place to side.

Instructions (sauce)

  1. Add olive oil to heated pan on medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and red pepper flakes, cook until garlic is slightly brown.
  3. Add tomatoes and salt, cook for around 5 minutes.
  4. Add meatballs, cook for 5 more minutes.
  5. Add basil, cook until basil is wilted.
  6. Add noodles, toss until coated.
  7. Garnish with pecorino romano and serve..
