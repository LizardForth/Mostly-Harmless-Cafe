White Wine Sauce

Good for about 1 lb of pasta.

Chicken and Tomatoes:

  • 300 g chicken
  • 1 package of cherry tomatoes (halved)

Sauce:

  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ¼ cup extra virgin olive oi
  • 5 Tbsp butter (unsalted)
  • ½ tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ cup white wine

Instructions:

If you're using this for pasta, go ahead and have that made. Remember to drain, chill, and reserve a bit of the pasta water in case you want to thicken the sauce.

  1. Cook chicken in pan on med-high heat.
  2. Midway through, add tomatoes
  3. Remove all (includeing sauce) once chicken finishes cooking.
  4. Reduce to medium heat and add olive oil with garlic and red peper.
  5. Cook until garlic turns brown.
  6. Add butter, cook until melted.
  7. Add wine, increase heat to med-high, and cook out alcohol.
  8. Add chicken, tomatoes, and sauce.
  9. Reduce heat to medium and cook for 5 minutes.
  10. Add pasta and toss.
  11. Garnish with pecorino romano and serve.
