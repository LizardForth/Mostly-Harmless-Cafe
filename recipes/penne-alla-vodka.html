Penne Alla Vodka

Pasta:

  • 1 lb penne pasta

Sauce:

  • ½ Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 100g pancetta/bacon
  • 1 shallot (minced)
  • 3 cups (2 cans) crushed tomatoes
  • ⅓ cup heavy cream
  • ⅓ cup vodka

Instructions:

Go ahead and make the pasta before starting the sauce (reserve ¼ cup pasta water)

  1. Add extra virgin olive oil to pan on medium heat.
  2. Add the pancetta/bacon, let simmer until all the grease has been sweated out.
  3. Add minced shallot, let sautee.
  4. Add tomatoes, let cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add cream, mix in.
  6. Add vodka, let alcohol cook out.
  7. Add pasta, mix in.
  8. If you want it thicker, add pasta water and stir in.
