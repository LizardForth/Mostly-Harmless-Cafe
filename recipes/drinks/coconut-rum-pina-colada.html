Piña Colada With Coconut Rum

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz coconut rum
  • 3 cups ice (less if you arent using a blender)
  • 4 oz pineapple juice
  • 3 oz cream of coconut
  • Slice of pineapple (garnish)

Frozen Piña Colada:

A bit less than 3 standard drinks.

  1. Add ice, rum, pineapple juice, and cream of coconut to blender.
  2. Blend on high. It should be smooth and frothy.
  3. Pour into glass garnish with pineapple slice and mini umbrella.

Shaken Piña Colada:

A bit less than 3 standard drinks.

  1. Add rum, pineapple juice, and cream of coconut over ice in cocktail shaker.
  2. Shake well(around 60 seconds works well).
  3. Strain into glass and garnish with pineapple slice and mini umbrella.
