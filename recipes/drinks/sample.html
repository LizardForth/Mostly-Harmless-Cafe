French 75

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz gin
  • ½ oz lemon juice
  • ½ oz simple syrup
  • 3 oz Champagne (or sparkling wine)
  • ice (mixing)
  • lemon wedge (garnish)

Simple Syrup (optional):

  • 1 part water
  • 1 part granulated sugar

Simple Syrup Instructions:

Completly optional you can easily buy this at a store, and its cheap. Will stay good for roughly a month if refrigerated.

  1. Add sugar and water to small sauce pan over medium heat.
  2. Stir until both have disolved.
  4. Let cool keep in sealed glass jar.

French 75 Instructions:

A bit more than 3 standard drinks.

  1. Combine all ingredients except champagne and ice in cocktail shaker.
  2. Add ice and mix well(Don't forget to close strainer lid).
  3. Strain out into glass(ideally a collins glass).
  4. Top with champagne and lemon wedge
