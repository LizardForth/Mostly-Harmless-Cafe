Lasagna

~| home

| food | pasta |

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • ½ lb ground pork
    • subst: ¼ lb sausage
  • 8 oz mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 small onion (diced)
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds (ground)
  • 6 oz can of tomato paste
  • 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 inch knob of ginger (minced)
  • 15 oz ricotta
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup pecorino romano
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese
    • not pre-shredded, cheaper is better, feel free to add more
  • salt & pepper
  • vegetable oil (for cooking meat)
  • olive oil (for cooking sauce)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Cook noodles per box instructions (don’t forget to salt water). Cool with cold water and set aside when done.
  3. Let simmer until it’s time to mix it in with the beef.
  4. In a large pot on medium-high heat, add a little vegetable oil and place ground beef and sausage. Mash the meat in and let rest for 1m30s. Flip and do the same with the other side.
  5. Add mushrooms, fennel seed, ginger, garlic, and onions to the ground beef. Cook until mushrooms are cooked and onions are translucent.
  6. Add tomato paste, stir until combined.
  7. Add in the crushed tomatoes. Let simmer for about 10 minutes.
  8. In a medium bowl combine the pecorino romano, ricotta, mozzarella, and egg with a generous amount of salt and pepper.
  9. In a 9½x11 baking pan, lay a fourth of your meat sauce mix followed by layer made by laying 3 cooked noodles.
  10. Next, add a fourth of the meat sauce, followed by a third of the cheese mix, followed by another 3 noodles.
  11. Repeat step 12.
  12. Put the remainder of your meat sauce followed by the remainder of your cheese sauce.
    • optional: garnish with additional mozzarella
  13. Cover and place in oven for 25 minutes.
  14. Uncover and place in oven for another 25 minutes.
  15. Remove from oven and let sit for 15 minutes.

Optional

  • More mozzarella on top, cheaper and low-moisture mozzarella has thicker texture than expensive mozzarella. Just don’t buy pre-shredded.
